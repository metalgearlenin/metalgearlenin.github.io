Metal Gear Lenin is a thrilling state of the art role playing game with its own set of story and characters. It is developed by a creative team of builders and programmers who appreciate the community and are not threatening to their community in any way.

    • The Story

    Metal Gear Lenin takes place in 1919 when the Gulag was first established under the Cheka. It would not be until 11 years until the camp population reached significant numbers.

    During the Gulag's first days in operation power goes to the wrong hands, the people are required to shoot the unlucky and needy or they fall to their fate of being locked in a camp. Only few dare to revolt...
